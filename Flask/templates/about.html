About the Project

This Employee Productivity Prediction platform leverages Machine Learning to forecast the actual productivity of garment workers and manufacturing lines. Our approach integrates multiple operational and environmental variables to create predictive models that provide actionable insights.

How It Works

  • Collects data on team performance, production metrics, and environmental factors.
  • Applies preprocessing and feature engineering for accurate model input.
  • Uses trained ML models to predict productivity levels in real-time.
  • Presents results via an easy-to-use web interface for quick decision making.

Benefits

  • Understand key drivers of productivity.
  • Anticipate low/high productivity days.
  • Optimize workforce and resource allocation.
